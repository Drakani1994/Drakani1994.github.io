2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to our website and all mobile applications published by Kibou Games.

3. Information we collect

a) Information collected automatically

When you use our Services, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system, language, device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (game progress, session length, interactions)
  • Crash reports and performance data

b) Information you provide

We may collect information you provide directly, such as when contacting us via email.

c) Information from third parties

If we use third-party services (such as analytics or advertising providers), these services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our Services
  • Analyze gameplay and performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Deliver advertisements (if applicable)
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent (e.g. for personalized ads or analytics where required)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g. improving game performance and stability)
  • Legal obligations

6. Advertising

Our games may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may use device identifiers and other data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your consent.

You can manage your consent preferences at any time within the app settings.

7. Analytics

We use analytics services to understand how players interact with our games and to improve the user experience.
We use Google Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google.
Firebase Analytics may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • Usage data (e.g. session length, gameplay events)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)

This data is used to analyze game performance, improve gameplay, and fix issues.

8. Advertising

Our games display advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a service by Google.
AdMob may collect and use the following data:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Interaction with advertisements

    • This data may be used to:
  • Show personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance

Personalized advertising is only used if you have given consent where required by applicable laws.
You can manage your ad preferences within the app or through your device settings.

9. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Google (Firebase Analytics, Admob)

10. International transfers

Some of our service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. In such cases, appropriate safeguards are applied in accordance with GDPR.

11. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

12. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

12. Children's privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

13. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure.
